Flan
====

Ingredients (makes 6)
-----------

* 1.5 cup sugar
* 6 large eggs
* 1 14oz can sweetened condensed milk
* 2 13oz cans evaporated milk
* 1 tsp vanilla

Instructions
------------

* preheat to 325
* carmelize 1 cup sugar and pour and coat the bottom of each ramekin
* blend eggs
* mix in milks
* mix in .5 cup sugar
* add vanilla
* bake for ~45'
* let cool for an hour
